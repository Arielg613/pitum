Simple as it gets

Three taps to a chavruta. That's it.

📖

Open Pitom

Tap "I want to learn." Pick any masechta, sefer, or parsha. No account setup. No profile to fill out. Just open and go.

📡

Go live

Your card appears to everyone on the app — your name, your city, what you're learning. Anyone can tap to join you instantly.

🤝

Learn together

You're connected in a live audio session right inside the app. No Zoom links, no phone numbers. Just two people and a text.

The idea behind Pitom
אוֹ חַבְרוּתָא אוֹ מִיתוּתָא
Talmud Bavli · Ta'anit 23a

The Gemara isn't being dramatic. Learning with someone is a fundamentally different experience — you're challenged, accountable, and you go deeper. The problem was never motivation. It was friction. Pitom removes that friction completely. Wherever you are, your next chavruta is one tap away.
